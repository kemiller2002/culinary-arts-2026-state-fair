# Sun God BBQ Sauce

## Main Recipe

### Ingredients

- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1/2 cup finely diced onion
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 cup tomato sauce
- 1/2 cup ketchup
- 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
- 1/4 cup honey
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 chipotle pepper in adobo, minced
- 1 tsp adobo sauce
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp orange zest
- 1 tbsp fresh orange juice
- 1 tsp unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1/2 cup water

### Steps

1. Heat oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Add onion and cook until soft and lightly golden, about 5 minutes.
3. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
4. Stir in tomato paste and cook for 1 minute.
5. Add remaining ingredients and stir well.
6. Bring to a gentle simmer.
7. Cook for 25–30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
8. Blend until completely smooth.
9. Pass through a fine-mesh strainer for a silky texture.
10. Refrigerate overnight before judging.

---

## Assembly

1. Bring sauce to room temperature 20–30 minutes before serving.
2. Stir thoroughly before placing in judging container.

---

## Notes

- The cocoa should not be identifiable as chocolate.
- Orange provides brightness and enhances the perception of the cacao.
- Honey softens the vinegar and creates a rounder finish.
- The goal is a familiar BBQ sauce with a deeper, more complex finish.
