# Nile Jewel Relish

## Main Recipe

### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Medjool dates, finely diced
- 3/4 cup pomegranate arils
- 2 tbsp dried hibiscus flowers
- 2 tbsp minced shallot
- 1 tbsp tamarind concentrate
- 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley, finely chopped
- 1 tsp fresh mint, finely chopped
- 1/2 tsp ground cumin
- 1/4 tsp ground coriander
- 1/4 tsp kosher salt

### Steps

1. Place the dried hibiscus in a small bowl and cover with hot water.
2. Let steep for 10 minutes, then drain thoroughly and finely chop.
3. In a mixing bowl, combine the dates, chopped hibiscus, shallot, tamarind concentrate, lemon juice, cumin, coriander, and salt.
4. Mix well and allow the flavors to meld for 20 minutes.
5. Fold in the parsley, mint, and pomegranate arils just before serving.
6. Taste and adjust salt or lemon juice as needed.

---

## Pistachio Dukkah

### Ingredients

- 2 tbsp pistachios
- 1 tsp sesame seeds
- 1/2 tsp coriander seed
- 1/2 tsp cumin seed

### Steps

1. Toast the pistachios, sesame seeds, coriander seed, and cumin seed in a dry skillet over medium heat until fragrant.
2. Cool completely.
3. Pulse or crush into a coarse mixture, leaving some texture.

---

## Assembly

1. Spoon the relish into a serving bowl.
2. Sprinkle the pistachio dukkah over the top just before serving.
3. Garnish with a few extra pomegranate arils if desired.

---

## Notes

- The relish may be prepared one day ahead.
- For best texture, add the pomegranate and dukkah shortly before judging or serving.
- The flavor should lead with tartness and citrus, followed by the sweetness of dates and the warmth of the spices.
- Hibiscus provides both color and a cranberry-like acidity that helps distinguish the relish from traditional fruit relishes.
